Shipping and Handling

To ensure that your package is properly delivered, and that you receive shipment within the time frame that we advertise, please make sure your address is fully entered and correct. Specifically, please use correct abbreviations and spacing! We unfortunately cannot take responsibility for incorrect addresses.

If you have entered the wrong address, please send us an email as soon as possible - as once the order is shipped, we can't make any changes to the shipping details.

Furthermore, if your tracking information states that your package was delivered but you haven't received it - please inform us as soon as possible.

We ship directly from our warehouse in China, and delivery takes between 9-18 business days from the date the order has been processed. In doing so, we're able to pass on the savings and offer our best prices, along with free shipping across our catalogue.

After your payment is authorized, it can still take 1-3 days to process your order. The delivery timeframe is an estimate and does not include weekends and public holidays.
